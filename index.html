My First VidCon by: the Tim Ridenour

In 2014 I was in the digital cinematography program at Full Sail University. I went to film school not to become an actor, or a director. I went to become a producer. To be a good producer you have to know how to do everything, and in film school you are required to do everything.

One of the requirements for the program is creating a youtube channel to post are homework assignment on. This could be anything from doing sound design, editing, or directing a commercial. I was fine with all of this, because I'm a behind the camera kind of guy. But I knew the time was going to come, that I had to step infront of the camera. I didn't have a choice about it. It was part of getting my degree.
